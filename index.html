About

Background

I'm an ICT Engineering master's student at the University of Padova, in the Life and Health curriculum, currently finishing my final exams. Before this I completed a Bachelor's in Information Engineering, with a thesis on modeling and control of the flight dynamics of a convertiplane.

What I've been working on

My focus has shifted toward E-Health and digital systems: classifying motor imagery from EEG signals with deep learning, and separately mapping how different brain regions talk to each other using phase-based connectivity metrics. I also did a group project designing a full relational database from scratch — from interviews and requirements to triggers and query optimization. I'm particularly interested in applying machine learning and network technologies to healthcare applications.
